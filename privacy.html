Tappadoo

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

Developer: Mirvan Studios (Mirvan LLC)

The short version: Tappadoo does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We built this app for our own toddlers, and we take their privacy (and yours) seriously.

What Data We Collect

None. Tappadoo does not collect any personal data, usage data, analytics, or device information. There are no accounts, no login, no registration, and no tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Tappadoo is designed for children ages 0–3 and their caregivers. We are fully committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since we collect absolutely no data from any user — including children — there is no personal information to protect, share, or delete.

Third-Party Services

Tappadoo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the app. We do not use cookies or any form of web tracking.

Local Storage

The app stores your sound and navigation preferences (such as sound on/off and swipe lock) locally on your device using standard browser storage. This data never leaves your device and cannot be accessed by us or anyone else.

Internet Connection

Tappadoo works entirely offline after installation. No internet connection is required to use the app, and no data is transmitted to any server.

In-App Purchases

Tappadoo may offer optional in-app purchases for additional book content. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or the respective platform. We do not process or store any payment information.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page with a new effective date. Given our commitment to collecting no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
